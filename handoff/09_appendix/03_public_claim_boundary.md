## Safe Public Claims

```text
The system supports panel-based AI/search response observability.
The system can generate proxy metrics for observed answer presence and source visibility.
The system helps structure brand truth, questions, concepts, evidence, and semantic pages.
The system includes methodology and caveat controls for benchmark reporting.
```

---

## Unsafe Public Claims

```text
Measures true AI market share.
Reveals hidden LLM preference.
Guarantees AEO/GEO ranking improvement.
Proves consumer preference.
Produces legally final competitive rankings.
```

Trust before commerce.
