---

## Use

```text
panel-based proxy measurement
observed AI/search-like response
observed answer share
within this probe panel
under this methodology
measurement period
confidence/volatility
limitations
```

---

## Avoid

```text
true market share
actual AI preference
hidden model preference
definitive AI ranking
guaranteed visibility
proves consumer preference
```

---

## Report Rule

Every report using observation metrics must include methodology and proxy caveat.
