## Domain Strategy

MVP intentionally covers three different semantic business patterns:

```text
K-Beauty: YMYL-lite trust/evidence/boundary/routine
Convenience Retail: local action/menu/promotion/comparison
Wedding: high-consideration vendor/package/contract/vibe
```

---

## Why These Domains

They demonstrate that the system is not a generic website builder only.

They prove:

```text
evidence and boundary handling
action and local intent handling
style/vibe and contract handling
competitive AI answer measurement
reporting and fix-it loop
```
